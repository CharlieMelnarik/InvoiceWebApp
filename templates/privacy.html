Privacy Policy

InvoiceRunner

InvoiceRunner helps businesses create invoices and estimates, manage client records, and send communications by email and SMS. This policy describes how information is collected, used, and shared.

Information We Collect

  • Account information for InvoiceRunner users (business users), such as username and email.
  • Client contact and billing information entered by business users, including name, email, phone number, and address.
  • Invoice and estimate records, line items, notes, and PDF documents generated within the app.
  • Technical/log information necessary for security, troubleshooting, and service reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the InvoiceRunner service.
  • To generate invoices/estimates and deliver them by email or SMS when requested by the business user.
  • To protect the platform, prevent misuse, and enforce service terms.

SMS Consent and Messaging

Clients provide SMS consent to the business using InvoiceRunner when they provide their phone number directly to that business for invoicing, estimate updates, payment reminders, or related service communication.

SMS consent is not sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing. Message frequency varies by business activity. Message and data rates may apply. Clients can reply STOP to opt out and HELP for assistance.

Email Communications

Business users may send invoices/estimates and related service communication by email to client addresses provided by the client or otherwise lawfully obtained by the business.

Service Providers

InvoiceRunner uses third-party providers to operate the service, including email and SMS delivery providers such as Twilio and SMTP/mail infrastructure, and payment processors such as Stripe.

Data Retention

Data is retained as needed to operate the service and support business recordkeeping, unless deletion is requested or required by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect data, but no online system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • Clients may opt out of SMS by replying STOP.
  • Business users can update or remove client contact information in their account.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the business that sent your message/invoice or reach support via the contact information published by InvoiceRunner.
